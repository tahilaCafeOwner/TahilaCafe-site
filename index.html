About Us

“Tahila” is a Hebrew word meaning praise, often expressed through singing and raising hands in worship. It speaks to joy, thanksgiving, and lifting our hearts to God.

We exist to be a cornerstone for our community, serving excellent coffee, food, and Christian products while creating a space for fellowship, music, and faith. Jesus is at the center of everything we do.
